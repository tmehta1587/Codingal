Hobbies

I love cafe hopping with my friends because it's a fun way to explore new places and try different foods and drinks. Ech cafe has its own unique vibes. My friends and I enjoy tasting new coffees, desserts and snacks. It is a fun way to relax and make memories together!

Watching

One of my favorite hobbies is watching K-dramas. I love how they combine romance, drama, comedy, and suspense, making every story engaging and unique. The characters are always interesting, and the emotional moments keep me hooked. Whether it's a heartwarming rom-com or an intense thriller, there's always something exciting to watch. I also enjoy how K-dramas take me into different settings and stories, making them a great escape from daily life. The soundtracks add to the emotions, and the actors bring their roles to life in a way that makes every episode enjoyable. Watching K-dramas is my way to relax and unwind. Whether I'm binge-watching a new series or rewatching a favorite, it's a hobby that never gets old.
