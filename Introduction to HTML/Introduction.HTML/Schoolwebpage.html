Wells International School


Hello! I am Tinashe Mehta and I am 17 years old! My nationality is Indian but I was born and brought up in Bangkok. Currently I study in Wells International School in Bangkok, Thailand. There are a total of four campuses spread throughout the city of Bangkok. Wells International School is known for its diverse community, emphasis on academic achievement, creativity and critical thinking in order to prepare students for opportunities in university. There are approximately 1000 students throughout the four campuses!

Courses

Wells International School provides both the AP and IB Diploma Programme providing students to opt for flexible and challenging academic options. The AP courses allow students to take college level classes and earn credits while on the other hand the IB Programme mainly focuses on academic excellence, critical thinking and global perspectives. The program requires students to chhose six subjects and three core components which are Theory of Knowledge, Extended Essay and Creativity, Activity, and Service.
